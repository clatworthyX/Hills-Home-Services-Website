Lawn care FAQs: choosing a mowing service

How do I pick a reliable mower?

Check local reviews, ask about insurance, and confirm they bring their own gear and dispose of clippings.

What should a mowing quote include?

Frequency, inclusions (edging, blowing, green waste), travel fees, and whether spraying or fertilising is extra.

Can I set-and-forget?

Yes—choose a recurring schedule with SMS reminders; pause during slow winter growth.

How to avoid scalping or weeds?

Keep mower blades sharp, adjust cut height seasonally, and treat weeds before they seed.

Do I need to be home?

No, if access is clear and pets are secure. Ask your mower to text a photo after each visit.
