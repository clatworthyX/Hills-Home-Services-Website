Tiling FAQs

How do I check a tiler’s credentials?

Ask for SA contractor licence and waterproofing qualification; request compliance certificates for wet areas.

What should be in a tiling quote?

Prep work, waterproofing scope, tile layout, trims, grout type/colour, movement joints and waste removal.

Do I supply tiles?

You can supply tiles; confirm overage (usually 10%) and who handles delivery.

How to minimise dust and mess?

Use drop sheets, plastic sheeting, wet-cutting, and daily clean-ups.

How long before I can use the area?

Most floors need 24–48 hours before traffic; showers typically 24 hours after silicone cure—confirm with your tiler.
