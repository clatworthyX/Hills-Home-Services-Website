Skip bin FAQs

What can’t go in the bin?

No asbestos, liquids, tyres, batteries, gas bottles or paints. Ask about mattresses—they’re often extra.

How do weight limits work?

Each size has a weight cap; heavy materials (concrete/soil) need clean-fill bins to avoid overload fees.

Do I need a permit?

Driveway placements don’t; verge or road placements may need council approval—ask your supplier.

How long can I keep the bin?

Commonly 3–7 days; extensions are available—confirm daily/weekly rates.

How to avoid extra fees?

Keep waste below the rim, separate heavy rubble, and cover the bin if rain is forecast to prevent added weight.
