Roller door FAQs

Why did my spring break?

Springs fatigue after set cycles; Hills temperature swings accelerate wear. Replace springs in pairs and re-balance the door.

Do I need regular servicing?

Yes—annual lubrication, tension checks and safety test of auto-reverse keep doors safe and motors lasting longer.

Can I DIY adjustments?

Avoid DIY spring or cable work—high tension is dangerous. Safe DIY: clean tracks, replace batteries, and clear photo-eyes.

Will a new motor fit my door?

Most modern openers retrofit; confirm door size, weight and headroom to choose the right motor.

How can I keep pests out?

Install new bottom seals and side brush seals, and keep the sill swept to block gaps.
