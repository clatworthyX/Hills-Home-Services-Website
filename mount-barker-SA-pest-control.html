Pest control FAQs: choosing right & staying protected

How do I confirm a pest tech is licensed in SA?

Ask for their licence number and verify it on the SA Consumer and Business Services register. Only licensed operators can legally apply restricted-use chemicals.

What should be in a treatment plan or quote?

Scope of pests treated, chemicals/products to be used, safety precautions for kids/pets, warranty period, follow-up visits and total price (including after-hours rates).

Which questions help me compare providers?

Ask: Do you provide a Timber Pest Certificate/Certificate of Compliance? What monitoring do you leave in place? How long have you treated homes in Mount Barker? What’s your earliest start and expected finish?

What are red flags?

Very low prices without detail, no licence or insurance proof, reluctance to provide chemical labels/SDS, pushy upsells for large prepayments, or no written report after treatment.

How do I keep pests away after treatment?

Seal gaps, trim vegetation off walls, store firewood off the ground, clean gutters, fix leaks, and keep food in sealed containers. Schedule annual termite and general pest inspections.

Should I get multiple quotes?

Yes—three quotes from licensed locals give a fair price range and reveal differences in products, warranty lengths and follow-up care.
