Painting FAQs: picking a pro & protecting finishes

How do I check a painter’s licence and insurance?

Ask for their SA building work contractor licence and public liability certificate; verify the licence on the SA Consumer & Business Services register.

What should a painting quote include?

Surface prep steps, number of coats, paint brands/lines, colour schedule, access needs, start/finish dates and warranty on labour and materials.

Which questions help me compare painters?

Ask about recent jobs in Mount Barker, who will be on-site, how they manage dust/fumes, and whether they use low-VOC paints for interiors.

What are red flags?

Cash-only with no receipt, vague prep description, no written scope, or unwillingness to show licence/insurance.

How do I look after fresh paint?

Avoid washing walls for 2–4 weeks, keep humidity low, and fix small chips quickly to prevent moisture ingress.
