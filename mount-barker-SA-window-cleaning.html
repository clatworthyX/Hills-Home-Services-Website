Window cleaning FAQs

How often should windows be cleaned?

Every 3–6 months outdoors; interiors can be longer depending on pets and cooking.

What is pure-water cleaning?

Filtered water leaves no minerals, so exterior glass dries spot-free without chemicals.

Do cleaners move furniture?

Most ask you to clear sills; they can move light items with care—confirm beforehand.

Will they work at height?

Yes, with poles or harness. Confirm access for ladders and that they carry height insurance.

Can I bundle services?

Yes—pair window cleaning with pressure washing or gutter cleaning to save a visit.
