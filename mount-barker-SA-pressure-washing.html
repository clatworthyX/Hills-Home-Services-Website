Pressure washing FAQs

Will high pressure damage surfaces?

Pros use adjustable pressure and tips; delicate surfaces get soft-wash with detergents instead of hard blasting.

What should be in the quote?

Surface list, square metres, chemicals used, water source, waste/runoff handling, and any stain pre-treatments.

Do I need to move anything?

Clear cars, furniture and pot plants; cover delicate gardens. Provide outdoor tap access.

How often should I pressure wash?

Most exteriors annually; shaded or tree-heavy areas may need more frequent cleaning.

Is water use compliant?

Operators should follow SA Water restrictions and use efficient equipment; ask if they can use tank water.
