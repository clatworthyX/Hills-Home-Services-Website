Cleaning FAQs: choosing the right service

What should a cleaning quote include?

Room list, task list, supplies provided, time estimate, frequency and any extras like oven or window cleaning.

Do cleaners bring their own products?

Most do; request eco or sensitive-skin options if needed. Confirm whether vacuums and mops are supplied.

How do I check trustworthiness?

Ask for police checks, insurance, ABN, and recent Mount Barker references. Choose providers that offer photos or checklists after each visit.

Can I reduce costs?

Declutter surfaces first, set a regular schedule, and group tasks into longer but less frequent visits.

Do I need to be home?

No, if access is arranged. Request a lockbox or agreed entry and a photo summary after the clean.
