Gutter cleaning FAQs

How often should gutters be cleaned in the Hills?

At least twice a year—after autumn leaf drop and before summer fire season.

Do I need gutter guards?

Useful near tall trees; still schedule annual checks for trapped dust and seed.

What should a quote include?

Roof access method, debris removal, downpipe flush, photos, and any minor repair pricing.

Are they insured for roof work?

Confirm public liability and working-at-heights certification before they start.

How can I reduce future blockages?

Trim overhanging branches, fit guards, and keep valleys clear during heavy leaf fall.
