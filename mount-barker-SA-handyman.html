Handyman FAQs: getting small jobs done right

What should I share when booking?

Photos, measurements, materials on-site, and preferred times help quote accurately and bring the right tools.

Fixed price or hourly?

Use fixed price for defined tasks; hourly can suit punch lists. Confirm minimum call-out time.

Licensing for handymen?

In SA, larger building work or specialist trades need the relevant licence—ask if any task requires a licensed trade (electrical/plumbing).

How do I check quality?

Request before/after photos, ask about warranties on workmanship, and check recent Mount Barker reviews.

Do I need to supply materials?

You can supply or request the handyman to supply; agree on brand/finish upfront to avoid mismatches.
